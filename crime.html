Crime & Mystery

Whether it's our human need to understand behaviors, a way to explore the dark world from the comfort of our own homes, or whatever the reason may be, there's no denying that crime documentaries/shows get us hooked and our blood pumping.
If you love crime and mystery shows as much as we do, then you've landed in the right place at the right time!

Breaking Homicide

Police Detective Derrick Levasseur and Forensic Psychologist Kris Mohandiewill look to bring closre to desparate families who are in search for justice following crimes committed against their loved ones. With the help of law enforcement and the victim's families, the detectives disect old and new evidence, follow any leads and chase down new clues in hopes of ending the search for justice once and for all.
You as the the viewer get to see the difficulties faced in investigations, sometimes even witness the moment potential suspects are made to squirm when provided with the evidence and faced with tough questions. Looks like there is finally a show to ease our anxiety about cold cases and stop the endless googling of investigation updates! If this is the show for you, be sure to check it out on Amazon Prime Video or the channel Investigation Discovery.



How To Get Away With Murder

Does the saying "why is your penis on a dead girl's phone?" ring a bell? No? Well that's probably because you are yet to get entangled in the 6-season rollercoaster journey with Annalise Keating and her ambitious law students. The criminal defence lawyer and professor teaches a group of aspiring law students when they become involved in a twisted murder that changes the course of their lives.
The viewers will witness the gang get through a series of of events that will have you screaming, laughing and just plain livid. Though fiction, you will very much get cuaght up and experience all the feels so luckily for you and I, part of this award-winning series is now available for our vewing pleasure on Netflix!



Blood Relatives

Is blood thicker than water? Does family come first? Certainly most would think so because as far as we're concerned, nothing beats family. Blood Relatives however, counters this notion as the 6-season docu-series lifts the mask hiding the most extreme of family secrets.
From betrayal to murder, the viewers get to witness just how, even the most seemingly perfect familes, can easily be torn apart resulting in murders committed within them. Blood Relatives is available for our viewing pleasure on Amazon Prime Video and the channel Investigation Discovery!
