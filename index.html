Anime

If you want to try something different Anime is the place to go! Orginiating in Japan, Anime is creates worlds of there own. Full of detailed storylines, in-depth fight scenes, intricate love stories, colourful backgrounds and animation. There is something from for everyone when it comes to anime. Here is a top 3 of Whats Your Pick personal favourites.

Naruto

Naruto is an iconic anime which has captivated the hearts of many since 2002. Many grew up watching such as masterpiece. Now don't let the 720 episodes dishearten you. Split between two parts, it follows the journey of Naruto, a kid with immense hidden power and his journey to become hokage of his village (and that is just the short version). Naruto is storytelling at its finest, with epic emotive subplots that build until epic battles and conclusions to the final arc following the 4th ninja war, Naruto has it ALL!
Storytelling? Check
Fight scenes? Check
Character development? Check
