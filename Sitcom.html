SITCOM

Knackered? No brain power? Then, sitcom is your best choice! Tune in to the best shows to get you cracking up... (WARNING: be prepared to split your sides when watching!)

Below are 3 quality sitcoms. Have a read to get started on a few or even to see if it is worth your time to re-watch it. But be aware! You are luring yourself into the dragon’s nest…

F.R.I.E.N.D.S

Classic. Anti-cinematic. Addictive. Join six reckless friends to bring you many joy and laughter. Live through the lives of the enchantress Rachel, highbrowed Ross, bonkers Phoebe, sarcastic Chandler, immaculate Monica and lovable Joey as they pass on their moments with you. It is a great way to finish your day off or start your overnight binge-watching with F.R.I.E.N.D.S! Get together with your family (or friends…) to share those hilarious moments.

THE OFFICE

An unusual one. Become absorbed in the mock-umentary of your frenzied colleagues through the fictional paper company. The peculiar and inimitable behaviour of each employee’s makes them amiable within a ‘supposed’ professional environment. Spend the night in with your loved ones preferably not with the children (contains inappropriate words and content) with this sitcom. Sit back and allow the interactions of the characters grab your attention and make you put down your phone.

MODERN FAMILY

Hilarious and relatable situations with families. The show is created with narratives of stereotypes and social conventions. Despite the seriousness of different central theme, the show is outright hysterical and witty. The families share their eccentricities and unconventional attitudes and still creates a warming atmosphere which makes you want to watch on.
